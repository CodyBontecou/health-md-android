HealthMD gives you unified access to all your health and fitness data from Health Connect.

FEATURES:
• View all your health metrics in one place
• Read data from Health Connect
• Export your health history
• Simple, clean interface

PRIVACY:
Your health data is yours. Health.md reads Health Connect data only for exports you request and never includes health data in campaign attribution. No third-party analytics or attribution SDK is used. For installs resulting from a Health.md campaign link, our first-party redirect service records the click and Android can send random app-generated install/event UUIDs plus validated campaign metadata to our first-party service. Raw referrers, Advertising ID, Android ID, device details, export content, accounts, and file paths are not sent.

PERMISSIONS:
HealthMD requests Health Connect permissions to display your health and fitness data. You control which data sources we can access.
