## Goal
Make Android usable on tablets/foldables with iPad-style adaptive layouts.

## Current gap
iOS has dedicated iPad sidebar/split-view screens. Android Compose screens appear phone-first with bottom navigation and single-column scrolling.

## Key files
- `app/src/main/java/com/healthmd/presentation/navigation/HealthMdNavigation.kt`
- screens under `app/src/main/java/com/healthmd/presentation/*`
- iOS reference: `HealthMd/iPad/*`

## Acceptance criteria
- Use adaptive navigation: bottom nav on compact width, navigation rail/sidebar on expanded width.
- Export/settings/history screens use two-pane or wider layouts where useful.
- Preview/history detail can show list + detail side-by-side on tablets.
- Works in portrait/landscape and foldable width classes.
- Accessibility and touch targets remain compliant.
